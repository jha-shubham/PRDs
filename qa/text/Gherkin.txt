Amazon Pay — Behaviour Specification (Gherkin format)

Feature: Amazon Pay checkout on a merchant site
  As a customer with an Amazon account
  I want to pay using my saved Amazon credentials
  So that I can complete checkout without re-entering card details

  Scenario: Successful one-click checkout with default payment instrument
    Given I am signed in to my Amazon account on the merchant site
    And I have a default saved card on file
    When I click the Amazon Pay button at checkout
    And I confirm the order on the Amazon Pay consent screen
    Then my order should be placed
    And I should receive an order confirmation email within 30 seconds
    And the merchant dashboard should show the transaction as Authorized

  Scenario: Checkout with an alternate saved payment instrument
    Given I am on the Amazon Pay consent screen
    And I have at least two saved payment instruments
    When I select a non-default card from the list
    And I tap "Place order"
    Then the order should be charged to the selected card
    And the consent screen should remember this selection for the session

Feature: Refunds and partial refunds
  As a merchant
  I want to issue full or partial refunds via Amazon Pay
  So that disputes can be resolved without manual reconciliation

  Scenario: Full refund within the refund window
    Given a captured Amazon Pay transaction less than 180 days old
    When the merchant initiates a full refund from the dashboard
    Then the customer should be refunded to the original payment instrument
    And the transaction status should change to Refunded

  Scenario: Partial refund exceeding the captured amount
    Given a captured transaction of $50
    When the merchant attempts a refund of $60
    Then the system should reject the refund with error REFUND_EXCEEDS_CAPTURE
    And no funds should move

Feature: Recurring payments
  Scenario: Customer cancels an active subscription
    Given a customer has an active Amazon Pay subscription
    When the customer cancels via the Amazon Pay account portal
    Then no further charges should be attempted after the current billing period
    And the merchant should receive a webhook event "billing_agreement.canceled"
