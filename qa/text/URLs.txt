Amazon Pay — Reference Document with URLs

Overview
Amazon Pay is a digital wallet checkout service. Full product page: https://pay.amazon.com . Developer documentation lives at https://developer.amazon.com/docs/amazon-pay-checkout/ and the API reference is at https://developer.amazon.com/docs/amazon-pay-api-v2/ .

Onboarding
Merchants register at https://sellercentral.amazon.com/ap/signin to obtain credentials. Sandbox dashboard: https://payments-sandbox.amazon.com . Production dashboard: https://payments.amazon.com .

SDKs
- JavaScript SDK — https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/01/EnglishCommonImages/AmazonPay/CheckoutV2/amazon.Pay.js
- Server SDKs — https://github.com/amzn/amazon-pay-api-sdk-nodejs , https://github.com/amzn/amazon-pay-api-sdk-python , https://github.com/amzn/amazon-pay-api-sdk-java
- iOS / Android — https://developer.amazon.com/docs/amazon-pay-checkout/mobile.html

Brand Assets
Button images: https://pay.amazon.com/help/external/201210370 . Brand guidelines PDF: https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/01/AmazonPay/Brand_Guidelines.pdf .

Webhooks
Configure your endpoint at https://sellercentral.amazon.com/payments/dashboard . Signature verification reference: https://developer.amazon.com/docs/amazon-pay-api-v2/signing-requests.html . Test deliveries from the sandbox: https://payments-sandbox.amazon.com/dashboard/notifications .

Compliance
PCI DSS overview: https://www.pcisecuritystandards.org/document_library . SCA / PSD2 guidance: https://developer.amazon.com/docs/amazon-pay-checkout/sca.html .
