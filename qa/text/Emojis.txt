Amazon Pay — Product Overview 🛒💳

🎯 Goal
Make checkout on partner sites as fast and trusted as buying on Amazon itself. ✨

👤 Who is it for?
- 🛍️ Returning Amazon customers who want frictionless checkout on third-party stores
- 🏪 Merchants who want higher conversion and Amazon-grade trust
- 🧑‍💻 Developers integrating payments into web and mobile apps

🚀 Key Features
- ✅ One-click checkout with saved Amazon address & card
- 🔒 Tokenized payments — merchant never sees the card number
- 🔁 Recurring payments via billing agreements
- 💸 Refunds (full and partial) up to 180 days post-capture
- 🌍 Localized payment methods per region
- 📊 Merchant dashboard with auth/capture/refund/dispute metrics
- 🎁 Amazon Gift Card balance applied automatically when available

📲 Customer Flow
1. 👀 Customer sees the Amazon Pay button on the merchant cart
2. 👆 Customer taps it
3. 🔐 Amazon shows the consent screen with merchant name + amount
4. ✅ Customer confirms
5. 📧 Order confirmation lands in inbox
6. 🎉 Done!

⚠️ Error Handling
- ❌ Card declined → show localized message, offer retry with another instrument
- 🚫 Three failures in a row → 5-minute lockout
- 🛠️ Webhook delivery failures → retry with exponential backoff for 24h ⏱️

🔐 Security & Compliance
- 🛡️ PCI DSS scope reduction for the merchant
- 🆔 Strong Customer Authentication (SCA) where required (EU 🇪🇺)
- 🔏 Signed webhooks; merchant MUST verify signature before acting

📈 Success Metrics
- ⬆️ +15% checkout conversion vs. card form
- ⬇️ -25% cart abandonment on mobile 📱
- 🕐 < 5s p95 authorization latency

🚧 Out of Scope (for v1)
- 🏦 Bank-direct (ACH) settlement to customers
- 🪙 Crypto wallets
- 🧾 In-store / POS terminals
